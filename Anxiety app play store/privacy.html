Last updated: June 2026
This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is completely protected.

About This App

AFSR Wellness built the Anxiety Personality Type Quiz as a paid wellness application. This page informs users of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. This app does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to use it.

All quiz responses, results, and personality type data are processed locally on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.

Email Report Feature

The app includes an optional feature to email your personalised results report to yourself. This feature is entirely optional and user-initiated. Your email address is used solely to send your requested report via EmailJS and is not stored, shared, or used for any marketing purposes.

Third Party Services

This app uses EmailJS solely for the optional email report feature. No analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies are used. No personal data is shared with any third parties for any other purpose.

Mental Health Disclaimer

This app is designed for self-discovery and general wellness purposes only. It is not a medical device, clinical assessment tool, or substitute for professional mental health advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are experiencing serious mental health concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Cookies

This app does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal information from any users, there is no risk to children's privacy.

Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal information beyond the optional email feature described above, there is minimal personal data at risk. The app functions primarily as a local wellness tool on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
